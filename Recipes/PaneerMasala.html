Panner Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • tsp cumin
  • tsp pepper
  • cloves
  • pods cardamom
  • inch cinnamon
  • tsp fennel / saunf
  • 20 cubes paneer
  • tsp chilli powder
  • tsp turmeric
  • tsp garam masala
  • tsp ginger garlic paste
  • tsp salt
  • 1tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • ½ cup curd (whisked)
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (crushed)
  • ¼ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Steps:

  • how to marinate and fry the paneer:
    1. firstly, in a large bowl take 20 cubes paneer.
    2. also add ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp garam masala, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, ¼ tsp salt.
    3. mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.
    4. now heat 1 tbsp ghee and marinated paneer.
    5. fry for a minute making sure all the sides are cooked well. keep aside.
  • how to make dhaba style spice powder:
    1. firstly, in a heavy-bottomed pan take 2 tsp coriander seeds, 1 tsp cumin, ¼ tsp pepper, 3 cloves, 2 pods cardamom, ½ inch cinnamon and ½ tsp fennel.
    2. dry roast on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
    3. cool completely, and blend to a fine powder. keep aside.
  • how to make dhaba style paneer masala curry:
    1. firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee and 2 tbsp oil.
    2. add 1 bay leaf, 2 pods cardamom, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 cloves and 1 tsp cumin.
    3. saute until the spices turn aromatic.
    4. now add 1 onion and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste. saute until the onions turn golden brown.
    5. further keeping the flame on low, add the prepared spice powder, ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder and ¾ tsp salt.
    6. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
    7. now add 2 tomato and saute until tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
    8. further, keeping the flame on low add ½ cup curd.
    9. stir continuously until the curd is combined well and oil separates out from the mixture.
    10. add ½ cup water or as required adjusting the consistency.
    11. now add fried paneer and mix well.
    12. cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.
    13. add 1 tsp kasuri methi, ¼ tsp garam masala and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.
    14. finally, enjoy paneer masala recipe dhaba style with roti, naan or phulka.
